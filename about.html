Who are we?

Rainydays was founded in 2012 by a group of avid outdoor enthusiasts who were tired of being caught in the rain without proper gear. They were driven to create a line of high-quality rain jackets that could withstand even the harshest weather conditions and pushing the comfort zone.

Our mission at Rainydays is to provide our customers with dependable rain jackets that offer both style and functionality. We use only the best materials, such as waterproof and breathable fabrics, to ensure that our products are of the highest quality. Our jackets are also designed with a variety of features, such as adjustable hoods and cuffs, to provide the ultimate protection from the rain.

Rainydays X earth

As a company, we are committed to sustainability and ethical practices. We use recycled materials whenever possible and ensure that our manufacturing processes are environmentally friendly. We also prioritize fair labor practices and work closely with our suppliers to ensure that everyone involved in the production of our jackets is treated fairly and with respect.

We only have this one earth, let’s care for it ♥
