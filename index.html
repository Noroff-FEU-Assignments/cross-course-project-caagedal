Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

I would first enlighten them on different security threats to understand why they would need good and trustworthy protection against it. After that I would advise them to take necessary steps such as sanitization of data, secure authorization and authentication and to make use of well-known third-party applications. And off course always do good research on the different methods beforehand to provide the best security possible.
